Mary Lane Cafe

Welcome to Mary Lane! We serve breakfast and brunch with all-natural, organic ingredients.
Pastries are baked fresh each day, as are our made-from-scratch croissants, breads,
and cakes. Our lunch meats are 100% natural and contain no chemicals or fillers.
Mary Lane strives to serve delicious meals with original, authentic tastes.
